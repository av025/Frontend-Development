Difference between Opacity and Alpha Channel

Opacity and Alpha Channel they do the same thing they both make our content transparent but Alpha Channel make transparent our colors and opacity will make transparent text , color , background-color , image, etc everything so we conclude that alpha channel will transparent on colors and opacity will transparent entire html element.
