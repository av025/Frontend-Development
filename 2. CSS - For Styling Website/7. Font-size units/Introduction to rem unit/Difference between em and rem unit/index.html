What is difference between em and rem unit???

Both em and rem are font-size units and mainly we are used for font-size property only.

Let we see the difference with the help of example.

Em unit for giving font-size

When we give font-size in em unit than there font-size was related to parent element's font-size and also calculated from parent element font-size only.
